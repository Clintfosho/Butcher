About us

Since 2010, Newstyle Butchery has proudly served Damofalls, Zimre, and surrounding communities. We specialise in meat processing services to a wide array of customers,We also process and supply bulk sausages and burger patties to small retail butcheries and restaurants.. Our mission is to provide exceptional quality and service while supporting local communities with a premier butcher experience. Planning an event or gathering? We are your trusted source for premium organic meats. Our Event Meat Supply service ensures that your special occasions are elevated with the finest cuts and flavors. From family celebrations to corporate events, our range of organic products will add a touch of excellence to every occasion. At Newstyle butchery, we're honored to be a part of your cherished moments.


Our vision is to ensure that we supply our customers with the highest quality of meats according to their specification, whilst also becoming the preferred butchery/meat shop for all the customers here in Ruwa in three years, and we have laid down plans and structures to enable us achieve this feat.
